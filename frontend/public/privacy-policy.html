Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

OpSyncPro.io ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Password (encrypted)

Connected Platform Data

When you connect third-party platforms, we access:

  • eBay: Listing data, inventory, pricing, order information
  • Amazon: Product information via Keepa API (public data)
  • Social Media (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube): Account connection status, posting permissions
  • OneDrive: Video files you explicitly choose to sync

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Time and date of visits
  • Device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Sync and manage your eBay listings
  • Post content to connected social media accounts on your behalf
  • Store and organize your product videos
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Respond to customer support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase: Database hosting with encryption at rest
  • Netlify: Application hosting with SSL/TLS encryption
  • OAuth tokens: Encrypted and stored securely; we never store your third-party passwords

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and regular security updates.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Third-party services that help us operate (Supabase, Netlify, payment processors)
  • Connected platforms: When you authorize actions (e.g., posting to TikTok)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Third-Party Integrations

Our service integrates with third-party platforms. When you connect these services:

  • You authorize us to access specific data as described during connection
  • Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs their data handling
  • You can disconnect any integration at any time from your Account settings

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Disconnect: Remove third-party platform connections at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Remembering your preferences

We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
