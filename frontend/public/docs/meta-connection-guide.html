Prerequisites

Before connecting, make sure you have:

  • A Facebook Page for your business (not a personal profile)
  • An Instagram Professional Account (Business or Creator account)
  • Instagram linked to your Facebook Page in Meta Business Suite

Step-by-Step Setup

1Convert Instagram to Professional Account

If your Instagram isn't already a Professional account:

  2. Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Account

  5. Select a category (e.g., "Shopping & retail")

Common Issues

"No Facebook Pages found"

This happens when Page access isn't granted during OAuth:

  • Make sure you're logged into the Facebook account that owns the Page
  • During the OAuth flow, look for "What Pages can this app access?" and select your Page
  • If you previously denied access, remove the app from Facebook Settings ‚Üí Business Integrations, then try again

"Instagram not linked"

This means your Instagram isn't connected to your Facebook Page:

  • Complete Step 2 above to link them in Meta Business Suite
  • Then disconnect and reconnect in Marketplace Central

"Request Couldn't be Processed"

This is usually a temporary Meta issue:

  • Wait a few minutes and try again
  • Make sure you're not hitting any rate limits
  • Check if Instagram has a commerce/shop connection that needs to be removed first
